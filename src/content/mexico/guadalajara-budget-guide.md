Guadalajara is the second most affordable World Cup host city (after Monterrey, slightly more accessible than CDMX) — accommodation prices are lower than in the capital, market and cantina food is extraordinarily cheap, and public transit costs 9.50 MXN per ride.

---

## Accommodation

**Budget**: Hostels (300–500 MXN/night in a dorm). Budget hotels in the Center: 700–1,200 MXN/night private room.

**Mid-range**: 1,200–2,800 MXN/night. Boutique hotels in the Historic Center and Colonia Americana. Hotel Morales (Centro, historic 1920 building) and similar properties offer excellent quality in this range.

**Upper end**: 2,800–7,000 MXN/night. Grand Hyatt Guadalajara and Camino Real are the references.

**Airbnb**: Apartments in Americana/Chapultepec: 700–1,800 MXN/night. Good option for multi-day stays.

**World Cup premium**: 40–60% increase over baseline rates during match weeks.

---

## Transit

**Tren Ligero / Macrobús**: 9.50 MXN per ride.

**Uber to [Estadio Akron](/[mexico](/mexico)/estadio-akron-guide)**: 120–200 MXN from the Center. Round trip: 250–400 MXN + possible surge post-match.

**Airport to Center**: 180–280 MXN by Uber; 380–450 MXN by official taxi.

---

## Food

**120–200 MXN/day eating well**:
- Breakfast: Goat birria + consommé at the market (80–120 MXN)
- Lunch: Torta ahogada + agua de Jamaica (50–70 MXN)
- Dinner: Al pastor tacos + tostada (60–90 MXN)

**350–600 MXN/day at restaurants**:
- One meal at La Chata or Birriería Las 9 Esquinas (150–250 MXN) + informal meals

---

## Attractions

**Free**:
- Historic Center and its plazas
- Metropolitan Cathedral (interior)
- Instituto Cultural Cabañas (exterior; interior has a fee)
- Parque Agua Azul
- Tlaquepaque (walking and viewing workshops)

**With admission**:
- Instituto Cultural Cabañas (Orozco murals): 100 MXN
- Regional Museum of Guadalajara: 75 MXN
- Guadalajara Zoo: 200 MXN
- Expreso Tequila (tequila region excursion): 1,500–2,500 MXN

---

## Sample Budgets

**Budget traveler (35–45 USD/day)**:
- Hostel: 450 MXN
- Food: 200 MXN (market, tortas, tacos)
- Transit: 30 MXN (Tren Ligero + walking)
- Attraction: 0 MXN
- **Total: ~680 MXN/day** (~34 USD)

**Mid-range (95–120 USD/day)**:
- Boutique hotel Centro: 1,800 MXN
- Food: 350 MXN (one restaurant + informal meals)
- Transit: 100 MXN (Tren Ligero + Uber)
- Attraction: 100 MXN
- Tips: 80 MXN
- **Total: ~2,430 MXN/day** (~122 USD)
