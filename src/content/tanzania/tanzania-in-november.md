November is when [Tanzania](/tanzania)'s short rainy season (vuli) arrives. Unlike the long rains (April–May), the short rains are briefer and less sustained — afternoon showers that last a few hours, followed by clear evenings. The landscape begins greening. The wildebeest are settling back into the Southern Serengeti ahead of calving season. And prices are at their shoulder-season low — some of the cheapest genuine safari opportunities of the year. [Zanzibar](/tanzania/zanzibar-guide)'s northeast trade wind arrives in November, beginning the beach season.

## Weather & Conditions

**Serengeti:** Short rains begin — typically afternoon showers of 1–3 hours. Mornings are usually clear and excellent for game drives. The grass is beginning to grow.

**[Ngorongoro](/tanzania/ngorongoro-guide):** Similar pattern — some cloud and afternoon rain. Accessible and still good.

**Zanzibar:** The northeast trade (kaskazi) arrives in November — Zanzibar's beach high season begins. The east coast is warming up. Zanzibar in November has improving conditions day by day.

**Mount [Kilimanjaro](/tanzania/kilimanjaro-guide):** November is one of the less reliable months — the transition to the short rains brings increasing cloud. The summit is achievable but conditions are less predictable than the main dry season.

## What to Do

**Pre-calving season, Southern Serengeti:** The wildebeest have returned to the Ndutu area ahead of the December–March calving season. November is when the plains are filling with pregnant wildebeest and the predators are anticipating the feast. The landscape is beginning to green from the short rains — a beautiful period photographically.

**Ngorongoro Crater (best value month):** November is perhaps the crater's best value month — dry season prices haven't returned, crowd levels are at annual minimum outside the April–May rains, and conditions are good. The crater is excellent year-round.

**Selous/Nyerere November:** The Rufiji River is rising with the first rains, and hippo and crocodile activity is changing. November in Selous has a transitional character that rewards patient naturalists.

**Zanzibar beach season opening:** November is the month Zanzibar's east coast fully opens. The northeast trade wind brings consistent conditions for kite surfing and creates the clean, warm water that makes the beaches world-class. Paje, Jambiani, and Michamvi are all coming into their peak season.

**[Dar es Salaam](/tanzania/dar-es-salaam-guide) as a city destination:** Tanzania's commercial capital is underappreciated as a destination. The Kariakoo market is East Africa's largest traditional market, the Msasani craft market is excellent for Tanzanian artisanship, and the Bongoyo Island marine reserve (ferry from Msasani) has good snorkeling. November is functional year-round city, not a seasonal matter.

## Festivals & Events

**Tanzanian Islands cultural events (November):** Stonetown and Zanzibar's coastal communities observe the full moon period with community events and traditional music in November.

**Kilimanjaro Marathon (if scheduled in November):** Several charity runs are organized around the mountain in the pre-rain window.

## Practical Tips

November safari prices are genuinely low — some of the year's most competitive rates. The short rains are not long-rain-level disruption; most game drives are feasible with morning scheduling and flexible afternoon planning.

Zanzibar November accommodation: the east coast properties are beginning to fill as the season opens. Book 4–6 weeks ahead for December and January dates — December is when prices spike for the northeast monsoon high season.

The short rains create dramatic photographic conditions — storm light, double rainbows over the plains, and the contrast of green grass emerging against the ochre Serengeti soil.

## Who November Is For

Value-first safari travelers who can accept intermittent afternoon rain for the year's lowest prices at some of Tanzania's best camps. Zanzibar beach season early arrivals who want November's uncrowded east coast before December prices and European visitors arrive. Pre-calving season aficionados who want to position themselves for January's drama.
